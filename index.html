Igatpuri is a beautiful town and a Hill Station in Nashik District in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The place is one of the best places to visit in Maharashtra in monsoon. Igatpuri is surrounded by the highest peaks in Sahyadri i.e. Western Ghats, most of them are forts built in Satavahana dynasty. It's heavenly for trekkers and hikers. Igatpuri is a place of significance in terms of Vipassana meditation.
